# Google SEO Ranking Guide for PDFMagic

## **Step 1: Submit Website to Google Search Console**

1. **Go to Google Search Console**
   - Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Add Your Website**
   - Click "Add a property"
   - Select "URL prefix"
   - Enter: `https://wwwpdfmagic.live/`

3. **Verify Ownership**
   - Choose "HTML file upload" method
   - Download the verification file
   - Upload it to your Railway project root
   - Add route in server.js:
   ```javascript
   app.get('/google123456789.html', (req, res) => res.sendFile(path.join(__dirname, 'google123456789.html')));
   ```
   - Click "Verify"

4. **Submit Sitemap**
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"
